Your Notifications — From Any Source.

Through our integration with Zapier, you're able to receive notifications from nearly 200 web services. Plus, we've got a killer REST API — so you can integrate your own awesome stuff.

A Feed for You. A Feed for Them.

You don't care about Sue's accounting notifications. And she doesn't care about your downtime notifications from Heroku. So we've built HookFeed to show only the notifications to which you subscribe — and nothing more.

Digestion.

Sometimes, it can be challenging to digest every digital bit thrown your way — so we've built in daily and weekly digests to lighten the load...and help you digest what's going on with your team.

Alerts — for the Important Stuff.

Shit! The website is down and traffic is spiking. Someone should let Milton in ServerOps know about this! What's that? HookFeed already did? SMS and Email alerts, delivered instantly to you, when important things happen.
